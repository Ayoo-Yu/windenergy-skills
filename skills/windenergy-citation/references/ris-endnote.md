# RIS, ENW, and BibTeX Notes

Use RIS for EndNote/Zotero interchange and BibTeX for LaTeX workflows.

Minimum RIS fields:

```text
TY  - JOUR
TI  - [Title]
AU  - [Author]
PY  - [Year]
JO  - [Journal]
DO  - [DOI]
UR  - [URL]
ER  -
```

For datasets or software that support a manuscript result, include repository,
version, DOI, and `[dataset]` or `[software]` labels when the target journal
requires them.
