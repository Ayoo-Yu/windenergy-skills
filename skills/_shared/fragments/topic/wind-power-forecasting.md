# Topic Fragment: Wind Power Forecasting

## Activation Condition

Load when the manuscript predicts wind power, wind speed, ramp events, or wind
farm time series.

## Scope

Use for forecasting data provenance, lead times, operational context, physical
drivers, and forecast evaluation.

## Allowed Content

Wind farm or turbine context, SCADA and meteorological inputs, lead-time
definition, power scaling, abnormal-operation filtering, ramp events, terrain,
wake context, and dispatch or reserve relevance.

## Forbidden Content

Named benchmark counts, named predictor conclusions, or one paper's table
requirements. Do not require uncertainty calibration diagnostics unless a
probabilistic forecasting fragment is also active.

## Output Influence

Connect forecasting accuracy or uncertainty to grid operation, market decisions,
maintenance, or reserve planning.

## Audit Influence

Audits may check whether data provenance, lead time, inputs, target, filtering,
and operational relevance are described when they are central to the claim.

## Examples

"State whether the forecast uses measured inputs, numerical weather inputs, or
a clearly described data source."

## Anti Examples

"Require four wind farms, four predictors, 24 lead times, or a fixed model
list."
