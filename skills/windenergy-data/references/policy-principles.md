# Policy Principles

Use current target-journal instructions first. For Renewable Energy and many
Elsevier energy journals, authors are expected to provide a Data Availability
statement and are encouraged to deposit/cite research data where feasible.

## Practical Rules

- State whether data are public, restricted, third-party, or generated by the
  authors.
- Give repository names, DOIs, URLs, licenses, and access dates when known.
- Explain restrictions clearly: operator NDA, commercial confidentiality,
  security-sensitive infrastructure, file-size constraints, or third-party terms.
- Cite public datasets that materially support the analysis.
- Keep code availability separate from data availability when possible.

## Author Responsibility

The author remains responsible for the accuracy of every data, code, and
repository claim. Flag missing repository records or unresolved permissions as
author actions.
