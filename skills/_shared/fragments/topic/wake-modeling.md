# Topic Fragment: Wake Modeling

## Activation Condition

Load when the manuscript studies wake effects, wind farm flow interaction,
turbine layout effects, or wake control.

## Scope

Use for flow physics, turbine layout, atmospheric conditions, model fidelity,
and validation.

## Allowed Content

Wake recovery, turbulence, atmospheric stability, layout geometry, inflow
conditions, turbine operating region, simulation fidelity, and field validation.

## Forbidden Content

Forecasting uncertainty diagnostics or conformal calibration tests unless those
topics are explicitly active.

## Output Influence

Connect modeling choices to physical interpretability and wind farm operation.

## Audit Influence

Audits may check whether physical assumptions, validation data, and operating
conditions match the claim.

## Examples

"A wake model should state the layout, inflow assumptions, and validation
basis."

## Anti Examples

"Wake modeling requires coverage backtests or interval calibration controls."
