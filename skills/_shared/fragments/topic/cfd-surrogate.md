# Topic Fragment: CFD Surrogate

## Activation Condition

Load when the manuscript builds or evaluates a surrogate for computational
fluid dynamics or high-fidelity simulation.

## Scope

Use for simulation fidelity, training data, physical constraints, generalization
domain, uncertainty, and runtime savings.

## Allowed Content

Simulation setup, governing variables, boundary conditions, mesh or solver
context, surrogate inputs and outputs, validation cases, runtime, and
out-of-distribution limits.

## Forbidden Content

Energy forecasting or conformal calibration checklists unless explicitly active.

## Output Influence

Present the surrogate as a scientific or engineering tradeoff between fidelity,
speed, and generalization.

## Audit Influence

Audits may check whether claimed speedups and accuracy are tied to comparable
simulation cases.

## Examples

"The paper should state which simulation regime the surrogate can emulate."

## Anti Examples

"A CFD surrogate must report wind farm forecasting lead times."
