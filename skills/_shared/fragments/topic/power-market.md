# Topic Fragment: Power Market

## Activation Condition

Load when the manuscript studies electricity markets, bidding, pricing,
dispatch, ancillary services, or market risk.

## Scope

Use for market rules, decision timing, risk, settlement, uncertainty, and
economic interpretation.

## Allowed Content

Market product, bid timing, price formation, imbalance, reserve, revenue,
risk measure, regulatory context, and participant perspective.

## Forbidden Content

Physical forecasting requirements or equipment-specific assumptions unless
another active topic supplies them.

## Output Influence

Frame claims around market decision value and rule compatibility.

## Audit Influence

Audits may check whether revenue, risk, and baseline comparisons match the
market design.

## Examples

"A bidding paper should connect method performance to settlement or imbalance
cost."

## Anti Examples

"Every market paper must include turbine-level sensor preprocessing."
