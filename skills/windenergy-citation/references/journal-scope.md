# Energy And AI Journal Scope

Use this file to decide whether a candidate paper is likely appropriate for a
wind-power, renewable-energy, power-system, or AI-for-energy manuscript.

## Core Energy Venues

- Renewable Energy
- Applied Energy
- Energy
- Energy Conversion and Management
- Renewable and Sustainable Energy Reviews
- Solar Energy
- Wind Energy
- Energy AI
- Energy and AI
- Journal of Cleaner Production, when the claim is about energy-system impact

## Power and Control Venues

- IEEE Transactions on Sustainable Energy
- IEEE Transactions on Power Systems
- IEEE Transactions on Smart Grid
- IEEE Transactions on Energy Conversion
- IET Renewable Power Generation
- Electric Power Systems Research
- International Journal of Electrical Power and Energy Systems
- Sustainable Energy, Grids and Networks
- Sustainable Energy Technologies and Assessments

## Method Venues

Use AI/ML journals or conferences only when the manuscript claim is about the
method, architecture, optimization strategy, or uncertainty estimator:

- Expert Systems with Applications
- Applied Soft Computing
- Engineering Applications of Artificial Intelligence
- Knowledge-Based Systems
- Neurocomputing
- Pattern Recognition
- Neural Networks
- Information Sciences
- IEEE Transactions on Neural Networks and Learning Systems

## Screening

- Prefer papers whose application domain is wind, solar, renewable integration,
  power systems, or meteorological forecasting.
- For wind-power forecasting claims, a generic time-series paper is only
  supporting evidence for the algorithm, not for energy-system performance.
- For physical claims, prefer studies that match the active profile's asset,
  measurement source, operating regime, and physical mechanism.
