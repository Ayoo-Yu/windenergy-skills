# Section: Abstract

## Multi-Journal Pattern

1. Problem and energy context.
2. Specific gap.
3. Proposed method or dataset.
4. Main quantitative results.
5. Practical or scientific implication plus boundary.

Keep the abstract factual and stand-alone. Use the target profile for the word
limit. If no journal is named, prepare a 200-word compact version and a
250-word standard version. Avoid citations and undefined abbreviations.
