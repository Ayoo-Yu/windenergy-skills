# Topic Fragment: SCADA Anomaly Detection

## Activation Condition

Load when the manuscript detects faults, anomalies, abnormal states, or
condition-monitoring events from SCADA or industrial time-series data.

## Scope

Use for labels, anomaly definition, operational consequence, class imbalance,
false alarms, detection delay, and interpretability.

## Allowed Content

Alarm definition, fault taxonomy, operating regimes, sensor provenance, missing
data, class imbalance, detection threshold, false positive cost, and maintenance
decision value.

## Forbidden Content

Forecasting interval diagnostics or market settlement rules unless active.

## Output Influence

Frame the paper around operational risk and actionable detection quality.

## Audit Influence

Audits may check whether anomaly labels, baseline rates, and false alarm costs
support the claim.

## Examples

"The paper should state what action an anomaly alert enables."

## Anti Examples

"Anomaly detection must use uncertainty interval calibration tests."
