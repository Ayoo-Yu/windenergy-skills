# Review Rubric

Use this rubric for manual evaluation of `windenergy-reviewer` outputs.

Pass conditions:

- Three reviewer reports are grounded in the same supplied manuscript facts.
- Every major criticism maps to a concrete author action.
- Technical risk, journal-fit risk, and missing evidence are separated.
- Unsupported claims are flagged without invented details.
- The synthesis explains likely rejection drivers and revision priorities.

Fail conditions:

- The output invents reviewer identities, journal decisions, line numbers, or
  experiments.
- It only gives generic praise or generic language editing.
- It hides missing data behind confident recommendations.
