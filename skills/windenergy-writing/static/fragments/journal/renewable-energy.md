# Journal: Renewable Energy

- Keep scope tied to renewable power generation, development, measurement, or
  application.
- For wind papers, foreground wind energy technology, forecasting, resource
  assessment, operation, or grid integration.
- Abstract limit: 250 words.
- Keywords: 1-6.
- Highlights: 3-5 bullets, each no more than 85 characters.
- References: no more than 50.
- Original research length: about 4000-6000 words excluding captions and
  references.
- Prefer restrained contribution language and clear limitations.
