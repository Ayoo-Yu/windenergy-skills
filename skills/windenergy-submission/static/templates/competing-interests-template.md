# Competing Interests Declaration Template

## Template 1: No competing interests

Save as `Competing_Interests.docx` and upload at the "attach/upload files" step.

```text
Declaration of Competing Interests

The authors declare that they have no known competing financial interests
or personal relationships that could have appeared to influence the work
reported in this paper.

Authors:
[Author A]
[Author B]
[Author C]
[Author D]
```

## Template 2: Some authors have competing interests

```text
Declaration of Competing Interests

The authors declare the following financial interests/personal
relationships which may be considered as potential competing interests:

[Author A] reports financial support from [Company/Organization] for the
research described in this paper. [Author B] serves on the advisory board
of [Organization], which is related to the subject matter of this paper.

[Author C] reports a patent application [Application Number] pending with
[Institution].

[Author D] and [Author E] declare no competing interests.
```

## Template 3: Editor/Board member affiliation

```text
Declaration of Competing Interests

[Author A] is an Editorial Board Member for [Target Journal]. This
author had no involvement in the peer-review of this article and has no
access to information regarding its peer-review. Full responsibility for
the editorial process for this article was delegated to another journal
editor.

All other authors declare that they have no known competing financial
interests or personal relationships that could have appeared to influence
the work reported in this paper.
```

## Filing instructions

1. Choose the appropriate template above
2. Fill in all bracketed information
3. Save as `.doc` or `.docx` format (NOT .pdf)
4. No signatures required
5. Upload at the "attach/upload files" step in the submission system
6. Also complete the online declarations tool during submission

## Competing interests checklist

Common interests to consider for energy research:

- [ ] Employment at a wind turbine manufacturer or energy company
- [ ] Consultancy agreements with energy sector companies
- [ ] Stock ownership in energy companies
- [ ] Patent applications related to wind energy technology
- [ ] Grants from energy industry (disclose even if already in funding section)
- [ ] Advisory board membership for the target journal or other journals
- [ ] Paid expert testimony in energy-related legal proceedings
- [ ] Personal relationships with editors or reviewers
