# Published Article Patterns

Use these patterns as energy-journal prose shapes, not as claims to copy.

## Problem Opening

`[Renewable asset/task] requires [capability], but [technical bottleneck] limits
[operational/scientific outcome].`

## Contribution Sentence

`Here, we propose [method] to [task] by [mechanism], using [data/evidence].`

## Results Sentence

`Across [dataset/scope], the proposed method [metric result] relative to
[baseline], with the largest gains under [condition].`

## Limitation Sentence

`The results are limited to [data/site/condition], and further validation is
needed for [deployment or generalization boundary].`
