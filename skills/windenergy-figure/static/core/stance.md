# Figure Stance

- Put panels in claim order: overview, comparison, mechanism, robustness.
- Use Times New Roman or a journal-compatible serif face when possible.
- Keep figure text at least 8 pt at final size.
- Use colorblind-safe palette choices and stable semantic colors.
- Show uncertainty when the claim depends on statistical stability.
- Keep captions conclusion-first and tied to site, horizon, metric, unit, target
  quantity, and sample period.
