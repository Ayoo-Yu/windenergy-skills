# Topic Fragment: Offshore Wind Siting

## Activation Condition

Load when the manuscript evaluates offshore wind locations, constraints,
planning suitability, resource potential, or spatial tradeoffs.

## Scope

Use for siting criteria, spatial data, constraints, uncertainty, and planning
decisions.

## Allowed Content

Bathymetry, distance to grid or ports, environmental constraints, resource
quality, exclusion zones, spatial resolution, stakeholder constraints, and
multi-criteria decision logic.

## Forbidden Content

Short-term forecasting checklists, online calibration tests, or model-specific
forecast metrics unless another active topic requires them.

## Output Influence

Frame the paper around planning tradeoffs and decision-relevant suitability.

## Audit Influence

Audits may check spatial data provenance, criteria weights, constraints, and
sensitivity.

## Examples

"A siting paper should explain why the selected criteria matter to planning or
deployment."

## Anti Examples

"Offshore siting must report forecasting lead-time diagnostics."
