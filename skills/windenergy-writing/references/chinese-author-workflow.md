# Chinese Author Workflow

Use this file when converting Chinese notes or paragraphs into English energy
journal prose.

## Workflow

1. Identify the technical subject, action, evidence, and limitation in Chinese.
2. Reorder long noun chains into English subject-verb-object structure.
3. Replace broad claims such as "significant improvement" with quantified
   results if provided.
4. Keep energy terms precise according to the active profile. Preserve supplied
   method names, units, variables, asset names, physical terms, and market or
   operational terms.
5. Add hedging only where evidence is limited.

## Common Repairs

| Chinese habit | Repair |
|---|---|
| Long background before the claim | Put the claim first, then context |
| "has important significance" | State the operational or scientific value |
| "prove" for model results | Use "show", "indicate", or "suggest" |
| Missing subject | Name the method, dataset, or result explicitly |
