# Paper Type Fragment: Deployment Paper

## Activation Condition

Load when the paper reports field deployment, operational integration, or
engineering adoption.

## Scope

Use for operational setting, stakeholders, constraints, monitoring, failure
handling, and value.

## Allowed Content

Deployment context, system boundaries, reliability, latency, maintenance,
operator decisions, risks, and lessons learned.

## Forbidden Content

Algorithm-specific checklists or topic metrics without a topic fragment.

## Output Influence

Foreground the operational problem and decision value before technical detail.

## Audit Influence

Audits check whether deployment claims are backed by real operational evidence
and stated boundaries.

## Examples

"The paper explains how the system changes a maintenance or dispatch decision."

## Anti Examples

"Deployment papers must use the same experiment layout as forecasting papers."
