## Bullet patterns by paper type

### Forecasting paper

- [Method name] achieves [X]% RMSE reduction in wind power forecasting
- [Module/feature] captures [specific pattern or event]
- Validated on [N] study units across [time period] of profile-relevant data

### Method/algorithm paper

- [Novel component] improves [metric] under [condition]
- [Architecture element] encodes [domain-specific pattern]
- Ablation study confirms each module's contribution

### Resource assessment paper

- [Method] achieves [X]% correlation with [reference measurements]
- Reduces [cost/time] by [X]% compared to [baseline method]
- Validated across [N] terrain types with [analysis method]

### Review paper

- Comprehensive review of [topic] covering [N] studies from [year]-[year]
- Identifies [N] key challenges in [field] for future research
- Proposes [framework/taxonomy] for [organizing the field]

## Saving

Save as `Highlights.docx` or `Highlights.txt` when the target journal requests
a separate highlights file.
