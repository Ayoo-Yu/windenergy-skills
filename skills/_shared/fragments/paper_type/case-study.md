# Paper Type Fragment: Case Study

## Activation Condition

Load when the paper deeply analyzes one or a small number of systems, sites, or
projects.

## Scope

Use for context, transferability, evidence limits, and practical interpretation.

## Allowed Content

Site description, study boundaries, data provenance, context variables,
decision relevance, and generalization limits.

## Forbidden Content

Broad universal claims or cross-domain rules without comparative evidence.

## Output Influence

Write from context to insight, then state what can and cannot transfer.

## Audit Influence

Audits check whether the case evidence supports the scope of the conclusion.

## Examples

"This case illustrates a condition that future multi-site work should test."

## Anti Examples

"A single case proves universal performance."
