# Journal Fragment: Energy

## Activation Condition

Load when the target journal is Energy.

## Scope

Use for broad energy-system framing, article depth, and journal-specific
submission expectations.

## Allowed Content

Energy conversion, operation, planning, system analysis, method relevance, and
profile-based limits.

## Forbidden Content

Applied Energy-specific thresholds or topic-specific diagnostics.

## Output Influence

Keep the framing broad, quantitative, and connected to energy-system impact.

## Audit Influence

Use the active journal profile for limits and required files.

## Examples

"The introduction should tie the technical result to an energy-system problem."

## Anti Examples

"Energy papers must satisfy Applied Energy-specific reference targets."
