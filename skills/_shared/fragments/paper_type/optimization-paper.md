# Paper Type Fragment: Optimization Paper

## Activation Condition

Load when the contribution is an optimization model, solver, scheduling policy,
market strategy, or planning formulation.

## Scope

Use for objective functions, constraints, uncertainty, feasibility, solver
behavior, and decision value.

## Allowed Content

Decision variables, constraints, objective terms, scenario design, feasibility,
optimality gap, runtime, sensitivity, and operational impact.

## Forbidden Content

Topic-specific physical or forecasting assumptions without an active topic
fragment.

## Output Influence

Write around the decision problem and tradeoff, then the formulation and
evidence.

## Audit Influence

Audits check whether optimization claims include feasible solutions, baselines,
and sensitivity where needed.

## Examples

"The formulation improves cost-risk tradeoffs under stated constraints."

## Anti Examples

"Optimization papers must use the same figure portfolio as empirical
forecasting papers."
