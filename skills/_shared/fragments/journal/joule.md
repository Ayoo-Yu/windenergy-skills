# Journal Fragment: Joule

## Activation Condition

Load when the target journal is Joule.

## Scope

Use for energy significance, strong graphical storytelling, and broad
interdisciplinary readability.

## Allowed Content

Energy impact, design principle, systems relevance, accessible mechanism, and
clear visual narrative.

## Forbidden Content

Unsupported breakthrough language or dense task-only framing.

## Output Influence

Make the main message memorable and tied to energy decisions or design.

## Audit Influence

Check whether the paper has a coherent high-level story and evidence boundary.

## Examples

"The contribution should read as a design insight or energy-system finding."

## Anti Examples

"A method leaderboard alone is a Joule-level narrative."
