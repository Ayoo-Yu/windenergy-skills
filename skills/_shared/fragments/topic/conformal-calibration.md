# Topic Fragment: Conformal Calibration

## Activation Condition

Load when the manuscript studies conformal prediction, adaptive conformal
methods, conformalized quantiles, exchangeability, online calibration, or
coverage guarantees.

## Scope

Use for conformal score definitions, calibration protocol, exchangeability
assumptions, online update timing, reliability diagnostics, and interval quality
interpretation.

## Allowed Content

Conformal calibration methods, coverage diagnostics, reliability and sharpness
tradeoffs, interval score, Kupiec and Christoffersen tests, matched-width or
matched-reliability controls, delayed-label handling, and method-level
reporting when the manuscript claims deployable method choice.

## Forbidden Content

Do not include any single paper's run count, wind farm count, predictor list,
specific test count, table number, reviewer patch, workspace path, or fixed
conclusion.

## Output Influence

Use cautious mechanism wording unless diagnostics isolate the explanation.
Distinguish family-level conclusions from method-level deployment advice.

## Audit Influence

If strong mechanism claims about interval expansion are active, audit for
interval score decomposition, matched-width comparison, and matched-reliability
comparison. If these controls are absent, allow empirical-signature wording and
block stronger wording.

## Examples

"A conformal calibration paper may need to explain the score, calibration
window, online update timing, and reliability-sharpness tradeoff."

## Anti Examples

"Require four wind farms, four predictors, 24 horizons, 3528 tests, GBR,
QRLSTM, or a specific static versus dynamic conclusion."
