# Journal: Renewable Energy

- Keep the manuscript within renewable power generation, measurement,
  development, or application.
- Use concise, factual wording for abstract and conclusion.
- Watch limits: abstract 250 words, 1-6 keywords, 3-5 highlights at 85
  characters each, and no more than 50 references.
- For wind forecasting, connect model behavior to wind-energy operation,
  physical constraints, or renewable integration.
