# Journal Fragment: Nature Energy

## Activation Condition

Load when the target journal is Nature Energy.

## Scope

Use for high-level energy significance, broad readership, concise structure,
and strong evidence visibility.

## Allowed Content

Field-level problem framing, broad implications, interdisciplinary readability,
and careful claim boundaries.

## Forbidden Content

Overstated novelty, dense implementation detail in the foreground, or
unsupported policy claims.

## Output Influence

Prioritize clear field-level stakes and concise evidence presentation.

## Audit Influence

Check whether broad claims are supported and whether technical detail is placed
where readers can use it.

## Examples

"The abstract should state why the finding changes an energy question."

## Anti Examples

"A narrow benchmark result automatically supports broad energy policy claims."
