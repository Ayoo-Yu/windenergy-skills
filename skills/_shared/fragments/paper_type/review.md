# Paper Type Fragment: Review

## Activation Condition

Load when the task is a review, survey, perspective, or taxonomy paper.

## Scope

Use for literature coverage, search protocol, taxonomy, synthesis, and gap
identification.

## Allowed Content

Search strategy, inclusion rules, evidence synthesis, thematic categories,
research gaps, and future research agenda.

## Forbidden Content

Invented citation counts, unsupported claims about consensus, or primary
experiment requirements.

## Output Influence

The writing should synthesize patterns rather than summarize papers one by one.

## Audit Influence

Audits check search transparency, coverage balance, and claim support.

## Examples

"The review separates mature evidence from emerging claims."

## Anti Examples

"A review must include experiments from the current author."
