# Writing Examples Index

Use these examples as structural patterns only. Fill task, metric, system,
method, and evidence terms from the active profile.

## Available Example Types

### Abstract

- Challenge to contribution template.
- Challenge to insight to contribution template.
- Multiple-contribution template.

### Introduction

- Niche task: task first, then applications.
- Familiar task: applications directly.
- General energy problem, then specific setting.

## Generic Opening Patterns

**Forecasting or prediction papers:**

```text
[Energy output or state] is difficult to predict when [physical, operational,
or market condition] changes. This creates risk for [decision]. The unresolved
question is whether [method or evidence type] can improve [decision-relevant
metric] under those conditions.
```

**Control or optimization papers:**

```text
[Energy asset or system] must balance [objective] against [constraint]. Existing
approaches often simplify [constraint or uncertainty], leaving a gap between
mathematical performance and operational feasibility.
```

**Resource assessment or siting papers:**

```text
[Planning decision] depends on reliable estimates of [resource or suitability
quantity]. Yet [data, spatial, temporal, or constraint limitation] makes the
decision uncertain.
```

## Contribution Phrases

```text
To identify when [method or design] changes [decision-relevant outcome]...
To separate [observed pattern] from [candidate explanation]...
To translate [technical result] into [selection, design, or deployment rule]...
To quantify the operating boundary under [condition]...
```

## Result Phrases

```text
The proposed approach improves [metric] by [value] under [condition], while
remaining bounded by [limitation].

The ablation shows that [component] accounts for [evidence], supporting the
claim that [narrow mechanism or design role].

The sensitivity analysis shows that the conclusion is stable across [range] but
weakens under [boundary].
```
