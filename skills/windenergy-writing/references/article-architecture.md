# Article Architecture

Use this file when rebuilding the full argument of a renewable-energy paper.

## Default Argument Chain

1. Energy-system problem: forecasting, operation, resource assessment,
   integration, or decision support.
2. Technical gap: data mismatch, physical inconsistency, uncertainty, scale,
   generalization, interpretability, or operational constraint.
3. Proposed approach: method, physics/AI coupling, data pipeline, or experiment.
4. Evidence: datasets, baselines, metrics, ablations, robustness checks.
5. Energy meaning: what changes for wind-farm operation, planning, or renewable
   integration.
6. Boundary: data scope, site dependence, limitations, and future work.

## Section Jobs

- Abstract: state problem, method, main quantitative result, and implication.
- Introduction: move from energy need to specific gap and contribution.
- Methods: make data, model, equations, and training/evaluation reproducible.
- Results: report performance and explain physical/operational behavior.
- Discussion: interpret why the result matters and where it may fail.
- Conclusion: summarize contributions without adding new claims.
