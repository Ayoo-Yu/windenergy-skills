# Paper Type Fragment: Control Paper

## Activation Condition

Load when the contribution is a controller, control policy, stability study, or
closed-loop operational strategy.

## Scope

Use for plant model, constraints, control objective, stability, robustness, and
closed-loop evidence.

## Allowed Content

Control objective, state and action definitions, constraints, robustness tests,
stability evidence, safety limits, and implementation timing.

## Forbidden Content

Open-loop forecasting checklists unless another fragment activates them.

## Output Influence

Clarify the controlled system, objective tradeoffs, and operational guarantees.

## Audit Influence

Audits check whether control claims match closed-loop evidence and constraints.

## Examples

"The controller improves a constrained operating objective under tested
disturbances."

## Anti Examples

"A controller is validated only by one open-loop prediction metric."
