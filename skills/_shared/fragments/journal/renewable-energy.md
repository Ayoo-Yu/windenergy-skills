# Journal Fragment: Renewable Energy

## Activation Condition

Load when the target journal is Renewable Energy.

## Scope

Use for renewable-generation relevance, technology focus, article depth, and
journal profile checks.

## Allowed Content

Renewable resource, generation, integration, design, operation, and application
value.

## Forbidden Content

Unrelated AI-only framing or another journal's hard limits.

## Output Influence

Make the renewable-energy application central to the argument.

## Audit Influence

Use the active journal profile for article type, length, figure, and submission
requirements.

## Examples

"A method claim should be tied to renewable energy performance or decisions."

## Anti Examples

"A generic machine-learning improvement is enough without renewable relevance."
