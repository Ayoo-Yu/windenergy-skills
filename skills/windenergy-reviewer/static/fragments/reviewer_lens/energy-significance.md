# Energy Significance Lens

Focus on:

- Operational decision value for wind farms, grid operators, markets, storage,
  forecasting, siting, maintenance, or renewable integration.
- Whether the manuscript explains who benefits from the result and under which
  operating regime.
- Whether the claimed contribution is stronger than the demonstrated evidence.
- Whether limitations are visible for geography, turbine type, data period,
  weather regime, or deployment environment.
