# Journal Fragment: IEEE TSTE

## Activation Condition

Load when the target journal is IEEE Transactions on Sustainable Energy or IEEE
TSTE.

## Scope

Use for power-system rigor, method reproducibility, engineering validation, and
IEEE-style structure.

## Allowed Content

Power-system relevance, mathematical clarity, reproducible algorithms,
engineering constraints, and comparative validation.

## Forbidden Content

Elsevier-specific submission sections or another journal's limits.

## Output Influence

Use precise technical framing and clear notation while preserving practical
power-system relevance.

## Audit Influence

Check notation, method detail, validation, and IEEE-style readiness.

## Examples

"A control or forecasting method should be tied to a power-system operating
need."

## Anti Examples

"IEEE TSTE uses Elsevier declaration and graphical abstract rules by default."
