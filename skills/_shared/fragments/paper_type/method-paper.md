# Paper Type Fragment: Method Paper

## Activation Condition

Load when the contribution is a new algorithm, model, workflow, or technical
procedure.

## Scope

Use for method motivation, reproducibility, fair comparison, ablation, and
failure modes.

## Allowed Content

Method assumptions, algorithm steps, parameters, complexity, baselines, ablation
logic, implementation details, and deployment constraints.

## Forbidden Content

Topic-specific metrics, journal-specific thresholds, or manuscript-specific
model lists unless another fragment provides them.

## Output Influence

Methods and Results must make the claimed advantage reproducible and fairly
tested.

## Audit Influence

Missing baselines, missing configuration, and unsupported efficiency or accuracy
claims become `SECTION_WARNING`, `UNCHECKED`, or `FAIL` depending on evidence.

## Examples

"The method section states the assumptions, input-output contract, and training
or solution procedure."

## Anti Examples

"Every method paper must use one fixed metric family or one fixed dataset type."
