# Topic Fragment: Storage Optimization

## Activation Condition

Load when the manuscript optimizes battery, hydrogen, thermal, or hybrid energy
storage operation or planning.

## Scope

Use for storage constraints, objective tradeoffs, uncertainty, degradation,
market or grid value, and feasibility.

## Allowed Content

State of charge, power and energy limits, efficiency, degradation, cycle cost,
reserve value, energy arbitrage, reliability, scenarios, and sensitivity.

## Forbidden Content

Wind forecasting or conformal calibration rules unless explicitly active.

## Output Influence

Highlight the decision tradeoff, such as cost, risk, reliability, emissions, or
asset degradation.

## Audit Influence

Audits may check whether constraints, objective terms, baselines, and
sensitivity support the optimization claim.

## Examples

"The formulation should show how storage constraints shape the operational
value."

## Anti Examples

"A storage optimization abstract should mention wind forecasting or interval
scores when the study does not."
