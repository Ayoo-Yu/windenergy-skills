# Paper Type Fragment: Resource Assessment

## Activation Condition

Load when the paper assesses renewable resources, site potential, spatial
patterns, or temporal availability.

## Scope

Use for resource definition, measurement or simulation basis, uncertainty,
spatial-temporal representativeness, and decision implications.

## Allowed Content

Resource metrics, site descriptors, uncertainty intervals, map validation,
seasonality, data provenance, and planning relevance.

## Forbidden Content

Forecasting-specific model diagnostics unless the active topic requires them.

## Output Influence

Frame the paper around planning decisions, resource uncertainty, and practical
site interpretation.

## Audit Influence

Audits check provenance, representativeness, uncertainty, and map or table
consistency.

## Examples

"The study links resource potential to siting or planning implications."

## Anti Examples

"Resource assessment must follow a forecasting benchmark checklist."
